The blockquote HTML element is used to designate blocks of text that are quotations from another source. When you use the blockquote tag, it typically indicates that the enclosed text is a longer quotation, set apart from the main content, and often indented for emphasis. Additionally, the blockquote tag is often accompanied by a cite tag to provide the source or attribution of the quotation.
